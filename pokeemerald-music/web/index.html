Pokémon Emerald — the soundtrack

the complete BGM, decoded from the pokeemerald source (MIDI + m4a voicegroups + DirectSound samples), synthesized live in the browser by an AudioWorklet reimplementing the GBA m4a sound engine.

Songs loop like on the GBA (loop points come from the [/] markers in the game's MIDI files). See README.md one directory up for how the engine works and how extraction was done.
